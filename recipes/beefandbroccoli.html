Homemade Beef and Broccoli Stir Fry

Here's a recipe to a hearty homemade beef and broccoli stir-fry made from your kitchen!

Ingredients:

  • 3 lbs Ribeye Steak
  • 1 broccoli crown, rinsed and chopped
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 1 red or green bell pepper (optional)
  • 1/4 cup reduced-sodium Kikkoman soy sauce
  • 3 tbsp honey
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 whole carrot, shredded
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp fish sauce
  • 2 tsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Prepare the Beef:
    Slice the ribeye steak thinly against the grain. This helps to tenderize the meat. Season the sliced beef with salt and pepper to taste.
  2. Prepare the Sauce:
    In a small bowl, mix together soy sauce, honey, minced garlic, fish sauce, rice vinegar, and sesame oil. Set aside.
  3. Prepare the Vegetables:
    Chop the broccoli crown into bite-sized florets. Chop the onion and bell pepper (if using) into thin slices. Shred the carrot using a grater.
  4. Stir-Fry:
    Heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the sliced beef to the skillet and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until browned. Remove the beef from the skillet and set aside.
  5. Cook Vegetables:
    In the same skillet, add the remaining tablespoon of olive oil. Add the chopped onion and bell pepper (if using) to the skillet. Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until they start to soften. Add the broccoli florets and shredded carrot to the skillet. Continue stir-frying for another 3-4 minutes until the vegetables are tender-crisp.
  6. Combine:
    Return the cooked beef to the skillet with the vegetables. Pour the prepared sauce over the beef and vegetables in the skillet.
  7. Final Steps:
    Stir everything together and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes until the sauce thickens slightly and coats the beef and vegetables evenly. Taste and adjust seasoning with salt and pepper if needed. Once everything is heated through and well-coated with the sauce, remove from heat.
  8. Serve:
    Transfer the stir-fry beef and broccoli to a serving dish. Serve hot over cooked rice or noodles.
